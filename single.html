café råg

student project from University of borås

Background

This project was part of my Web Content Manager & Designer education. Main focus during the whole project was user experience. It did however involve many different steps since the goal was to deliver a complete and functioning website, optimized for the unique users a.k.a the customers of the café.

About the client

RÅG is a stylish retro café here in Uddevalla that makes it feel like you travel back in time when entering through the front door. The idea behind the café is to offer regular "Swedish fika" as well as real prepared food from sustainable sources with a high availability of vegan alternatives.

The café had its premiere opening in november 2019 and has since then successfully been using Facebook and Instagram for spreading news and inspiration.

Problem insights

The natural flow of social media like Facebook and Instagram makes it harder for the client to keep relevant and important information visible, structured and easily findable at all times.

A majority of users (customers) are women above 50 years according to the client. Many of these are also retired. With this in mind there is quite some uncertainty about the way this group uses the internet.

Purpose & Goals

The purposes and goals of a website are to:

  • reach a larger customer base
  • offer a structured and easily findable way to advertise menus, open hours and directions for new customers to find their way
  • ability for users to book a room for having conferences and events
  • ability for users to order cakes and other delicacies online
  • showcase the unique environments of the café

Solutions

Functional
  • Secure encrypted data transfers with https connection for the safety of the customers as well as SEO.
  • Choice of platform: Wordpress
  • Webshop solution: WooCommerce
  • Mobile navigation: Hamburger menu + custom fixed bottom menu
Non-functional
  • Give easy access to the most common tasks such as viewing the menus and open hours.
  • Highlight the environmental aura of the place by using colors and patterns from the café.

Time of delivery

The website is to be delivered on June 7th 2020 (www.råg.se).
